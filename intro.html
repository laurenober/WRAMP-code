Imagine yourself participating in a game in which you pick a marble out of hat full of colored marbles. Some marbles are pink, and the other marbles are blue. There are two outcomes in this game; if you draw a pink marble, you win, but if you draw a blue marble, you lose.



In this experiment, the arc chart/graph, shows the proportion of pink/blue marbles in the set. The bigger the portion of pink arc is, the more pink marbles there are in the set and your chance of picking one will vary accordingly.
